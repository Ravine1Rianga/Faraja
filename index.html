How It Works

Up and running in 3 simple steps

No technical skills required. Faraja is designed to be used by any family member, even from a phone.

Register in 60 seconds. Enter the deceased's details, set a fundraising goal, and invite your committee members by phone number.

Coordinate with Dignity

Manage vendor bookings, task assignments, and the program of service — all from your phone. The platform stays online as a permanent tribute.

About Faraja

Born from a Kenyan Family's Grief

Faraja means "comfort" in Swahili. We built this platform after our own family struggled to coordinate a funeral across three counties and the diaspora — managing M-PESA payments on spreadsheets, chasing vendors without reviews, and printing the program at midnight.

We believe every Kenyan family deserves to grieve with dignity, not logistics stress. Our mission is to become the most trusted partner for families in their hardest moments.
